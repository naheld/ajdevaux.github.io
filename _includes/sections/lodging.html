Lodging

The wedding will be held in greater Orlando, about one hour from Disney/Universal and from New Smyrna/ Daytona beach. If you'd like to stay near the festivities, we suggest the following:

  • The Alfond Inn , Winter Park
    A Boutique hotel near a lovely walking/shopping area.

  • Hilton Orlando North , Altamonte Springs
    Closer to the action and in a good location for reaching nearby attractions and the airport.

We also encourage you to check out hotels in downtown Orlando, and AirBnBs which are very affordable in our area. St. Stephen's Catholic Church is only a 30 minute drive from the Orlando International Airport or Downtown.

Unfortunately Central Florida does not have great public transportation, and we highly suggest renting a car. Let us know if you need help finding a ride!
